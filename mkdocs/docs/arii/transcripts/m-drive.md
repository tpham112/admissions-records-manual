# Move Transcripts to M Drive & Distribute

## Location

Go to the **SAC — eTrans Download** folder in the M Drive.

**Directory:** `SAC -- eTrans Downloads`

---

## Steps

1. Log received transcripts to student records in Colleague via the **IASU screen**.
   - See [Logging Transcripts in IASU](iasu-logging.md) for detailed steps.

    !!! info "Correction"
        Use **RECD Official Received** instead of PEND Pending.

2. Move the downloaded transcripts to the **'Parchment'** and **'XAP'** folders.

3. Check the **'JST Military Trans'** folder for new transcripts — look in the **"Import to Laserfiche"** sub-folder.
   - Rename files to the same format as Parchment and XAP, then log into IASU.

4. Check the **admissions@sac.edu** folder for new transcripts.

5. **Distribute transcripts** from `admissions@sac.edu`, `JST Military Trans`, `Parchment`, and `XAP` to:
   - **AR III** — College and JST transcripts
   - **AR II** — High School transcripts
   - Distribution is by Alpha Breakdown.

6. Copy & paste transcripts from `admissions@sac.edu`, `Parchment`, and `XAP` folder 1 **"Import to Laserfiche"** to the **'QF — Import to Laserfiche'** folder.

    !!! warning
        Do **NOT** put them in the **"ARCHIVES"** folder.
        **JST Military Trans** transcripts do **NOT** go in the QF folder.

7. Move transcripts from all source folders (folder 1 "Import to Laserfiche") to folder 2 **"Downloaded Completed"**.
