# New Staff Onboarding

## Access

New staff members need the following system access set up before they can begin processing:

- Colleague (Ellucian)
- Laserfiche / Perceptive Content (ImageNow)
- CVC Platform
- Self-Service / Dynamic Forms
- M Drive access

Contact your supervisor to submit access requests through IT and HR.

## Training

Training for new staff should cover the following areas:

- Colleague navigation and key screens (see [Colleague Pages Reference](colleague-pages.md))
- FERPA compliance and student privacy (see [FERPA](ferpa.md))
- Processing procedures for your assigned role (ARI, ARII, ARIII, or AR Tech)
- Laserfiche scanning and document management
- Enrollment Verifications workflow
- Residency determination basics

!!! tip
    Review the [ListServ](https://www.caccrao.org/caccrao-listserv/) for ongoing professional development resources and communications from CACCRAO.
