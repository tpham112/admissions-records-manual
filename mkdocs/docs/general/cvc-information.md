## Do students need to request their Exchange class transcripts be sent to their Home College?

Students wanting to use the Exchange must consent to have their transcripts automatically shared from the Teaching College to the Home College at the end of the term. The Exchange informed consent statement at time of enrollment specifies that a student must consent to having their transcripts automatically shared with the Home College. This consent acts as the request for any classes taken through the Exchange.

## Classes started today, but my pre-reqs haven't been cleared yet. Am I able to still enroll?

**NO.** All registration for CVC stops the day the class begins.

If a CVC student is still interested in registering for a course that has already started, then they must:

1. Fill out a "regular" student application via CCCApply
2. Complete a "Student Change of Information" to deactivate their `SAC.CVC.ND` program in SPRO.

## Pre-Requisite Clearance

- Upload transcript to CVC for pre-requisite clearance.
- We only process transcripts through the **CVC platform**.

---

## CVC Students Who Want to Switch to SAC as a Regular Student

1. Verify the student has **not registered for any courses** through CVC yet.
    - If they are currently in classes, they must finish out the term.
    - If classes have not yet started, they must **drop the CVC courses** before registering for SAC courses.
2. Have them apply to Santa Ana College as a regular student.
3. Have them submit a **Change of Information Request** to turn off their CVC program.

## Can CVC Students Submit EW Requests?

**Yes.** They will need a PDF request since they will not have access to Self-Service for Dynamic Forms.

---

## Can CVC Students Request Pass/No Pass?

**Yes.** They must speak with a SAC counselor for signature on a PDF Form before submitting to us or have the counselor submit the Dynamic Form on their behalf (*student must know SSO login information to use Dynamic Form*).

---

## Can CVC Students Receive Add Authorization?

**No.** CVC students cannot receive Add Authorization.

---

## Can CVC Students Update Their Home Campus?

**No.** CVC students cannot update their home campus.

!!! tip
    If the request is related to financial aid, recommend the student update their FAFSA to include SCC instead of switching their home campus to SAC.

---

## Can CVC Students Be Reinstated After Dropping/Being Dropped?

**Yes.** Email the instructor of the course and ask them if they would be willing to reinstate the student. Attach an Instructor Change Form for them to fill out their portion of the reinstatement. Have the student sign as well. Once all the signatures are collected, process the reinstatement.

---

## Can CVC Students Be Added Manually After Dropping By Accident? (Before Census)

**Yes.** Email the instructor of the course and ask them if they would like to grant Add Authorization to the student. If the instructor grants Add Authorization, send the student an Add Card (PDF) and have them fill out and send back. Register the student and notify [dmiranda@cvc.edu](mailto:dmiranda@cvc.edu). Make comments in Colleague to indicate the student was added manually after dropping by accident.
